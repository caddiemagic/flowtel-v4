# Medicine Wheel Specification

The Flowtel Medicine Wheel is a luxury cream-and-gold compass that gently points guests back toward center.

## Structure

- Background: warm cream or very light ivory.
- Wheel centered inside its card with generous padding.
- No large outermost ring.
- Two concentric gold rings around the day numbers.
- No thin extra ring inside the number ring.

## Number Ring

- 28 day markers.
- Day 28 displays as `28+`.
- All markers are equally spaced around the circle.
- Day 1 sits directly below WEST.
- Day 28+ sits directly above WEST.
- Days count counter-clockwise from Day 1 toward SOUTH, EAST, NORTH, and back to WEST.
- Day circles should be larger than tiny dots, but never touch.
- Fill: white or very light cream.
- Border: warm gold.
- Number text: dark brown.
- Typography: elegant serif.

## Active Marker

- The active day keeps its blush highlight.
- A gold star/diamond marker hovers precisely over the active cycle day.
- Marker should be centered over the active day circle, not offset.
- The marker should feel like jewelry, not a warning icon.

## Cardinal Directions

Display:

- NORTH
- EAST
- SOUTH
- WEST

Rules:

- Directions sit outside the number ring.
- Directions remain inside the card boundary.
- Directions should be subtle, small, elegant, and dark brown.
- Directions should never crowd the day numbers.

## Inner Seasons

Season labels sit in the four corners surrounding the wheel:

- Top Left: Inner Autumn, Days 20–26
- Top Right: Inner Summer, Days 12–19
- Bottom Right: Inner Spring, Days 6–11
- Bottom Left: Inner Winter, Days 27–5

Optional icons:

- Autumn: gold maple leaf
- Summer: gold sun
- Spring: pink rose
- Winter: gold snowflake

## Center Rose Compass

The center is a gold rose compass.

It should include:

- Compass rose geometry
- 8 primary points
- North and South longer than East and West
- Diagonal points shorter and more delicate
- Blooming rose spiral at the center
- Layered petals and subtle linework

It should feel:

- Feminine
- Timeless
- Boutique spa
- Sacred geometry
- Luxury illustration

Avoid:

- Four gold pillars
- Cartoon rose icons
- Overly mystical clutter
- Heavy dark backgrounds

## You Are Here Legend

Below the wheel:

- Gold diamond/star icon
- `YOU ARE HERE`
- Helper copy: `Click on a day to see your previous visits below.`

The legend must sit below SOUTH and must not overlap any cardinal label.
