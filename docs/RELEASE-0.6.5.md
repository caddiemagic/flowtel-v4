# Flowtel Release 0.6.5 — Cycle Tracker + Concierge Polish

## Changes
- Updates public Cycle Tracker styling to better match Flowtel branding while preserving the existing questions, flow, logic, and data outputs.
- Ensures the Cycle Tracker button reads “My Cycle Data” and reliably enables after selecting a date.
- Adds the Queendom invitation after cycle data is revealed or a day is selected.
- Simplifies the Concierge Desk header by placing practitioner identity directly under the Concierge Desk title.
- Replaces the repeated “Today you’re tending…” header text with a rotating daily affirmation.
- Adds “You’re on day X and clocked into the X Wing” to Today’s Flow.
- Removes the small Awaiting Turndown and Checked Out stat cards.
- Moves New Connections and Your Clients into the compact stat card row.
- Highlights the main Turndown Service queue when there are assigned items waiting.
- Limits service queue counts/items to the practitioner’s assigned wing.
- Adds checkout confirmation items to the service queue with a Clean Room action.
- Logs room care with: “[Level] [Name] lovingly prepared your room for your next visit.”

## Install
Replace the included files in your repo, then commit and push.
